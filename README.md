# 🌿 Touch Grass

Welcome to **Touch Grass**, the ultimate web application designed to help you reconnect with nature without ever leaving your screen! 

If you've been told to "go outside" or "touch some grass," you've come to the right place. We've brought the great outdoors directly to your browser. 

## 🌟 Features

- **Dynamic Environment:** The grass isn't just green; it's alive! Depending on your real-world location, the background changes dynamically to match your local time. Whether it's the crack of dawn, high noon, a beautiful sunset, or the dead of night, your grass adapts. (Powered by the awesome [SunriseSunset.io](https://sunrisesunset.io/))
- **Interactive Sparks:** Click anywhere on the grass to create delightful, magical sparks! It's like fireworks, but smaller, safer, and infinitely more satisfying.
- **Milestone Tracker:** We keep track of how many times you've touched grass. Reach special milestones (50, 100, 500...) to unlock secret, snarky messages. Can you reach the mythical 10,000,000 mark? (We dare you.)
- **Persistent Progress:** Your grass-touching progress is saved locally. Close the tab, take a break, come back, and your click count will be exactly where you left off. 

## 🚀 How to Run

Running **Touch Grass** is easier than actually going outside:

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open the `index.html` file in your favorite web browser.
3. Allow location access (if prompted) so the app knows whether it's daytime or nighttime in your area.
4. **START TOUCHING GRASS!** Just click, click, and click away.

## 🛠️ Behind the Scenes

Built with pure vanilla web magic:
- **HTML5:** For the basic structure.
- **CSS3:** For that sweet, sweet Inter font and the mind-blowing `cubic-bezier` transitions.
- **JavaScript (Vanilla):** To handle geolocations, calculate sunrise/sunset times, create particle effects, and judge you for how many times you've clicked.

## 💡 Why did we build this?

Because sometimes, the real world is too far away, and the Wi-Fi is too good.
